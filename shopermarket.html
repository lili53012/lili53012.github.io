Descriptions

Shoepermarket is a business to business to consumer (B2B2C) solution that provides a dedicated one stop application for shoe wears for our customers. With all the shoe listings from different vendors /retailers, it eliminates the need for our customers to manually source and compare prices from different platforms.

It was a group project that was done in a group of four during my year 2, semester 2. I was tasked to work on vendor's listing management which allows vendors to sell their products on our website (JavaEE). Besides that, for the customer side (Angular), I was tasked to work on the browsing of products and viewing of product details.
