Descriptions

The project was done in a team of 4. The objective of this project is for us to apply what we have learned in class about database systems and develop a web-based database application based on the requirement given by the professor.

FDS basically offers food delivery service to the general public. It allows restaurants to list their food items on the website. Customer will order from the website and each order will be assigned to a delivery driver. There are full-time and part-time delivery driver and they have different kind of schedules. There are promotion campaigns listed by the restaurant staff.

Technology/Language Used

PostgreSQL, Laravel PHP, Boostrap 4, JavaScript
